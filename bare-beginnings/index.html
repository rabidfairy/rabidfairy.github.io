Bare Beginnings...

"Anyone can cook but only the fearless can be great!"

Auguste Gusteau says this to Remy, the mouse, when he is having one of his many hallucination episodes , in one of the best movies that Pixar ever produced. Ratatouille. I've never had Ratatouille. It looks delicious , though. I know this blog is too cutesy and it doesn't have a lot of edge. Also, I cant really cook that well. The thing that I can assure you of is that nobody gets food poisoning when I cook for them. Plus the name HungryMomo is really misleading in that sense. I only look like a momo, but I can't really make one.

So, this is me , trying to be fearless, trying to learn both cooking and writing about it; trying to be 'Julie' to a couple of "Julia"s. Yeah, I watch a lot of movies. I am also learning a thing or two about Photography so that I can take better pictures of the things that I rustle up.

I have literally never held a chicken like that, if that is chicken. Also I've only cooked chicken once in my life, which I managed to both overcook and undercook Â at the same time. So, I have a long way to go.

Despite my lack of experience , I am very passionate about cooking. It excites me. And I come from the land of turmeric and a current population of 1,359,278,293 people. We have cows on our streets ( and thats where they poop), and cow pee stored in tin cans in most houses (not mine!). And despite the very unsanitary picture that I just painted , my country is very beautiful. It is rich in culture and the most amazing ingredients and recipes; some of which I will be trying to make.

Thats all for now folks!

Aishwarya Puranik
